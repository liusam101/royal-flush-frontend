Provably Fair & RNG

🎲
Certified RNG

Our Random Number Generator is certified by iTech Labs (Certificate #ITL-2024-001847) and tested to NIST SP 800-22 standards. The RNG produces statistically random, unpredictable card sequences with no patterns or bias.

🔗
How Provably Fair Works

Before each hand, the server generates a seed hash and publishes it. After the hand, the seed is revealed so you can independently verify that the deck order was fixed before any action was taken — proving no manipulation was possible.
